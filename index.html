Training Process
MMA has its own unique set of protocols, rules and battle tactics. In its current form it is one of the more technical and demanding sports in existence, as it requires from its fighters a tremendous amount of skill, heart and determination.
Sports Camp
At our Sports Camp for MMA, you will have an opportunity to improve your skills in any martial art and even to mix several fighting styles, learn new techniques, and master the ones you already know. The camp opens every summer season.
Championships
MMA competitions follow a unique tournament format by which competitors are selected by draw to determine who they will face in each round of competition. They have nine weight classes for men and now five for women.
